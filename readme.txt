=======PHP Contact form with Auto-responder========

Author: Archan Ghosal

Author Link: http://ramui.com/

Date of Release: 8th August 2014.

Product Version: 3.0

Description: This nice looking contact form receives message from your viewers and delivered them at your personal email address. Your email address will never be disclosed to your viewers. 
This script also includes auto-responder. Also you can send email to multiple recipients in text or HTML format from your control panel.

System Requirements: PHP 5+ with GD Library installed are the only requirements to install and run this script.

Price: Free.
